## Example 3: Technical Documentation

<Visibility for="humans">
  <InteractiveAPIExplorer />
</Visibility>

<Visibility for="agents">
  <h2>API Documentation</h2>
  
  <h3>Authentication</h3>
  <p>All API requests require authentication using an API key. Include your API key in the request header:</p>
  
  <pre><code>
  Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY
  </code></pre>
  
  <h3>Available Endpoints</h3>
  
  <h4>GET /api/v1/users</h4>
  <p>Retrieves a list of all users in your organization.</p>
  <p><strong>Parameters:</strong></p>
  <ul>
    <li><code>limit</code> (optional): Number of results to return (default: 50, max: 100)</li>
    <li><code>offset</code> (optional): Number of results to skip (default: 0)</li>
    <li><code>role</code> (optional): Filter by user role (admin, member, guest)</li>
  </ul>
  <p><strong>Response:</strong></p>
  <pre><code>{`{
  "users": [
    {
      "id": "usr_123",
      "email": "user@example.com",
      "name": "John Doe",
      "role": "admin",
      "created_at": "2024-01-15T10:30:00Z"
    }
  ],
  "total": 150,
  "has_more": true
}`}</code></pre>
  
  <h4>POST /api/v1/documents</h4>
  <p>Creates a new document in your workspace.</p>
  <p><strong>Request Body:</strong></p>
  <pre><code>{`{
  "title": "My Document",
  "content": "Document content here",
  "folder_id": "fld_456",
  "tags": ["important", "draft"]
}`}</code></pre>
  <p><strong>Response:</strong></p>
  <pre><code>{`{
  "id": "doc_789",
  "title": "My Document",
  "url": "https://app.example.com/docs/doc_789",
  "created_at": "2024-03-26T14:20:00Z"
}`}</code></pre>
</Visibility>

  <h3>How do I get started?</h3>
  <p>Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account at https://example.com/signup. No credit card required for the trial period. Once registered, you'll be guided through our onboarding wizard which takes about 5 minutes to complete. You can invite team members immediately or explore the platform on your own first.</p>
  
  <h3>What payment methods do you accept?</h3>
  <p>We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and can also arrange invoice-based billing for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount compared to monthly billing.</p>
  
  <h3>Can I change my plan later?</h3>
  <p>Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll only be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and you'll retain access to your current plan's features until then.</p>
  
  <h3>Is my data secure?</h3>
  <p>Absolutely. We take security very seriously. All data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption and in transit using TLS 1.3. We are SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and HIPAA compliant for healthcare customers. Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS in geographically distributed data centers with automatic failover and daily backups.</p>
  
  <h3>What kind of support do you offer?</h3>
  <p>Support varies by plan. Starter plan users receive email support with 24-48 hour response times. Professional plan users get priority email and chat support with 4-8 hour response times.   <h3>Do you offer a free trial?</h3>
  <p>Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on all paid plans. No credit card is required to start your trial. You'll have access to all features of the plan you choose during the trial period. If you decide not to continue after the trial, your account will automatically revert to our free tier (limited to 2 users and 1GB storage).</p>
